How to Report Problems

Your voice can spark real change in your community.

Step 1: Document

  • Take clear photos
  • Record the exact location
  • Describe the type of damage

Step 2: Contact the Right Office

  • Roads & Bridges: DPWH or Engineering Office
  • Water Systems: Barangay or Water District
  • Schools: DepEd Division Office
