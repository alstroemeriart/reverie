About Infrastructure Watch PH

Building a safer, more transparent future—one barangay at a time.

Every Filipino deserves roads that don’t flood, schools that don’t leak, water systems that don’t break, and public projects that are finished—not forgotten. Infrastructure Watch PH was founded to make sure these basic needs are no longer ignored.

📌

Who We Are

A student-led advocacy initiative created for CC 100, dedicated to monitoring public infrastructure across the Philippines. We are not part of the government— we are citizens who care.

🎯

Our Mission

To track real infrastructure challenges from national highways to remote barangay roads, and empower communities to speak up, report issues, and demand accountability.

📢

Why We Exist

Flooded streets, broken bridges, unpaved roads, and unfinished government projects impact everyday lives. We exist to amplify these stories and push for solutions—not excuses.

🏘️

Why Barangays Matter

True national progress begins with the smallest communities. A project in a remote barangay can change more lives than a giant structure in a city.

Our Core Values

Transparency

We believe people deserve clear and honest information about public projects.

Community First

Every voice matters—especially those living in far-flung areas.

Action

Awareness is good, but change happens when communities take a stand.

“Development isn’t real until it reaches the last barangay.”
